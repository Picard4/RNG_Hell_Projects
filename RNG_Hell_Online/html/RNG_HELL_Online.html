RNG HELL Online

Developed by Palmarino DiMarco to extract your sanity, or whatever is left of it if you played the first game. Available since December 2020
Special Thanks to Helen Katalifos for helping me develop the game along with everyone who playtested and gave me advice for various elements of the game.

THE BASICS

When the game begins, your character will spawn in the center of the screen surrounded by enemies and opposing obstacles.
In order to win this game, you must use random attacks to kill every last one of the enemies while avoiding the opposing obstacles.
If you lose all your HP or get OHKOed, you will lose the game.
You will not survive in this game without a good amount of luck. Touching the items around the map may increase your luck if you need more.

THE CONTROLS

It's recommended that you try to forget the controls of the first game to avoid muscle memory issues.
The arrow keys are used to move. In this release you can even move diagonally!
Use the W A S D keys for attacking. More will be explained about them in the attack section.
Press Z or the Spacebar to warp to a random space. You'll need luck for the warp to be successful...
Press Escape to commit die.

ATTACKS

There are 4 attacks in this game, with the one you use being randomly selected whenever you attack.
Enemies have a 50% chance of dodging your attacks.

The first attack is a Melee attack. It has a 50% chance of triggering and can be used to destroy items or enemies.
The key you enter when attacking decides the direction of the melee attack. W sends it up, A sends it left, S sends it down and D sends it right.

The second attack is a Range attack. It has a 30% chance of triggering and will spawn a supporting obstacle.
The supporting obstacle's spawning location and direction will be completely random, and it will turn to the enemy's side if it hits an opposing obstacle or an enemy dodges it.

The third attack is a Shield. It has a 10% chance of triggering and causes your character to look like this
Shields last for a short time, but they make you impervious to opposing obstacles while they last.

The fourth attack is a mystery. It has a 10% chance of triggering and it... does a thing.

TIPS

In this game, elements can only interact if they have the same shape.
If you get a shield while you already have one, the countdown for your shield expiring will reset.
If you do not have a shield, the colour of your circle will depend on your HP
Whenever you are hit, you will be warped to a random space. You may get comboed from this if you're unlucky.
Taking your time is incredibly cringe, so you may lose a point for each second that passes.
This game was mainly playtested on Firefox, so try switching to it if you have problems running the game
Raise the volume and wear headphones to enhance your RNG HELL Online experience, or lower the volume if you're a casual who prefers to keep their hearing.
